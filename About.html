About the System

The ICT Help Desk & Asset Management System is a web-based application developed to improve the management of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) resources within the CSIR-Savannah Agricultural Research Institute (SARI). The system provides a centralized platform for managing ICT assets, recording maintenance activities, handling support tickets, and reporting equipment faults in an efficient and organized manner.


Purpose of the System

The primary purpose of this system is to replace manual record keeping with a digital solution that improves efficiency, accuracy and accessibility of ICT information. It enables the ICT Department to monitor equipment, respond to support requests quickly, and maintain complete records of maintenance activities.


Problems Solved by the System

  • Eliminates manual recording of ICT assets.
  • Prevents loss of maintenance and repair records.
  • Improves the management of ICT support requests.
  • Provides faster access to ICT information.
  • Allows staff to report equipment faults online.
  • Stores image and video evidence of reported faults.
  • Supports better planning and decision-making.
  • Improves accountability within the ICT Department.

How to Use the System

Dashboard

Displays a summary of all ICT assets, help desk tickets and maintenance records stored in the system.

Assets

Register ICT equipment, edit asset information, search for assets, delete records and export asset information into CSV format.

Help Desk Tickets

Create support tickets submitted by staff, assign priority levels, monitor ticket status, resolve completed tickets and delete unnecessary records.

Maintenance Records

Record maintenance activities by entering the Asset ID, fault description, action taken and technician responsible for the repair.

Upload

Staff members report faulty ICT equipment by completing an online fault report form. Users can attach image and video evidence to help technicians identify the problem before maintenance begins.

Reports

Displays all submitted fault reports. ICT staff can view uploaded evidence, delete individual reports or clear all reports from the system.


System Workflow

  1. Register ICT assets in the Assets page.
  2. Staff report faults through the Upload page.
  3. The ICT Department reviews submitted reports.
  4. Support tickets are created and managed.
  5. Maintenance activities are recorded after repairs.
  6. The Dashboard automatically updates system statistics.
